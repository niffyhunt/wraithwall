# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want WraithWall's community spaces — issues, pull requests, and direct contact — to be free of harassment and technical bad faith.

## Expected behaviour

- Be direct and technically honest
- Respect maintainer time and scope boundaries
- Credit sources and avoid misrepresenting production status
- Do not demand immediate responses as if this were a staffed vendor support desk

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Publishing non-consensual private information
- Deliberate disruption of issues or security reporting channels
- Coordinated attacks against the live deployment presented as "security research" without prior agreement

## Enforcement

Reports: contact@wraithwall.online with subject `CoC report`.

The maintainer may remove content, close threads, or block repeat offenders. There is no separate moderation committee — this is a solo-maintained project.
